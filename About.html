    • NDIBWAMI MUGISHO ALLIANCE

    I was born in the Democratic Republic of Congo, a country where war was widespread and underdevelopment was its main consequence. Growing up in such circumstances, change became the one thing I desperately wanted to see, live, and experience. I had to be bold in order to pursue my dreams in an environment that kept telling me I could never be anything more than a victim or a coward. Instead, that struggle became my source of motivation and the beginning of my passion for technology. Many people pursue technology because it sounds cool to be called a software engineer or web developer, but for me, it is an exit door from what I have always known, and a path toward the future I want to see in my community.
